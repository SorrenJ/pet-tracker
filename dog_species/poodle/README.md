# Poodle

## Description

The Poodle is an elegant, highly intelligent breed that originated in Germany as a water retriever, though it became the national dog of France. Poodles come in three sizes — Standard, Miniature, and Toy — but all share the same breed characteristics. Renowned for their hypoallergenic curly coat, exceptional intelligence, and athletic ability, Poodles consistently rank among the smartest dog breeds. They excel in obedience training, agility, and even truffle hunting. Beneath their sophisticated appearance lies an active, fun-loving dog with a keen sense of humor.

## Life Span

- Standard: 10 - 13 years
- Miniature: 12 - 15 years
- Toy: 14 - 18 years

## Diet

Poodles need a high-quality diet matched to their size variety. Their active nature and fast metabolism require consistent, nutrient-dense meals.

- **Puppies (up to 12 months):** 3-4 meals per day with size-appropriate puppy formula; toy and miniature puppies may need more frequent small meals to avoid hypoglycemia
- **Adults (1-8 years):** 2 meals per day; Standard: 2-3 cups, Miniature: 1-1.5 cups, Toy: 0.5-0.75 cups of dry food daily
- **Seniors (8+ years):** 2 meals per day with senior formula adjusted for lower activity
- **Key nutrients:** High-quality proteins for lean muscle, biotin and omega fatty acids for coat health, antioxidants for immune support
- **Avoid:** Artificial colors and fillers, excess grains, and overfeeding toy/miniature varieties
